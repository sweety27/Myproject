About Us

GrabTees is a start up company situated in Bangalore,India which deals in bulk order of both promotional and designer T-shirts.We accept orders for custom digital printing on team t-shirts, sports jerseys, college t-shirts, company t-shirts, promotional t-shirts, eco friendly tees, political t-shirts, music t-shirts, party t-shirts, event t-shirts and many other. We also under take job orders for e-commerce companies and shops for printing customized t-shirts.We accept any kind of fabrics for fulfilling your printing needs.You can provide your designs or we can do it for you. You can just mail designs to us and we will do the custom printing on clothes for you.We develop samples as per your specification and proceed through the final printing process only after your approval.We print all your orders at approximately 2-3days which may exceed depending upon the complexity of the designs.The delivery time will depend upon the destination where the orders are needed to be delivered. However we try our best to deliver it to our customers at the least possible time.We also provide an inclusive pricing system which do not include any hidden charges.We take customer satisfaction very seriously.Our motto is to achieve customer satisfaction through innovation, competitiveness and Quality of Service.
